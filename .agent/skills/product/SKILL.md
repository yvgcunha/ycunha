# Product Management Skill

Use this skill when planning features, analyzing requirements, or discussing the "Why" behind a task.

## Product Thinking
- **User Centricity**: Every feature should solve a specific user pain point or provide direct value.
- **MVP vs. Premium**: Always clarify if the goal is a quick prototype or a polished, production-ready feature.
- **Prioritization**: Use frameworks like MoSCoW or RICE to suggest what to build first.
- **Feedback Loops**: Suggest ways to gather telemetry or user feedback for iterative improvement.

## Strategy
- Break down complex requests into logical, shippable phases.
- Ensure the "Personal Hub" and "Portfolio" align with a professional personal brand.
- Suggest "delight" features that go beyond basic functional requirements.
